Gameplay Mechanics

In Infinite Dungeon, players navigate through various levels, solving puzzles, defeating enemies, and discovering hidden treasures. The game emphasizes strategic decision-making and exploration.

Objectives

Your main objective is to navigate through the dungeons, overcoming obstacles and challenges. Collect resources, find keys, and unlock new areas as you progress.

Levels and Progression

The game consists of multiple levels, each presenting unique challenges and puzzles. Players can advance to higher levels by completing objectives and gathering resources.

Tips for Players

  • Pay attention to your surroundings for hidden items.
  • Use the environment to your advantage during challenges.
  • Experiment with different strategies to solve puzzles.
