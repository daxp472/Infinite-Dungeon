Basic Controls

  • Movement: Use W, A, S, D to move
  • Interact: Press E to interact with objects
  • Attack: Left click to attack enemies
  • Jump: Press Space to jump
  • Inventory: Press I to access your items

Game Objectives

In this game, you will navigate through various challenges and puzzles. Here are the key goals:

  • Solve puzzles to unlock doors and progress to the next level
  • Collect items to aid in your journey
  • Avoid or defeat enemies (coming soon)
